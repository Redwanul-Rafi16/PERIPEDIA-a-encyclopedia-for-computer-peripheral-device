About Peripedia

Academic Project for CSE Peripheral & Interface Systems Course.

Project Objective

Peripedia is an educational encyclopedia structured to assist computer science students and electronics enthusiasts in learning the engineering components, working principles, applications, and performance capabilities of primary peripheral devices.

By presenting technical characteristics in structured specifications tables and comparing subtypes side-by-side (like GSMarena matrices), users can make informed design decisions regarding hardware architectures.
